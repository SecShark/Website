    Trainings

    Our courses are designed for information security freshers who are keen to take a initial step further into the world of cyber security, we aim to train individuals to face real world cyber security challenges as well as provide deep knowledge into cyber security, it's workings and granular insights.

    Cybersecurity BootCamp

    A CEH understands attack strategies, the use of creative attack vectors, and mimics the skills and creativity of malicious hackers. Unlike malicious hackers and actors, Certified Ethical Hackers operate with permission from the system owners and take all precautions to ensure the outcomes remain confidential.

    OSCP Training

    We provide exam-specific and practical penetration testing experience through live instruction, lab tutorials, and custom lab environments inspired by real-world situations.You get to learn from OSCP instructors while completing the exact types of tasks as on the OSCP exam

    CompTIA Security+

    Our CompTIA Security+ Boot Camp teaches you information security theory and reinforces that theory with hands-on exercises to help you learn by doing. You will learn how to configure and operate many different technical security controls — and leave prepared to pass your Security+ exam.
